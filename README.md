Overview

This repository contains a Tableau workbook that consolidates restaurant transaction data into a single interactive dashboard. It is designed to help restaurant managers and analysts monitor category-wise revenue contribution, evaluate the relationship between customer ratings and revenue, track category ranking trends over time, and identify top revenue-generating customers.

Key Metrics Tracked


Average by Quantity – Average quantity ordered per transaction
Sum by Discount – Total discount applied across all transactions
Sum by Unit Price – Total unit price value across all transactions
Category by Revenue Contribution – Revenue share generated by categories A, B, C, and D
Revenue Distribution by Menu Items – Revenue breakdown across menu sub-categories Type1, Type2, and Type3
Customer Rating vs Revenue Analysis – Relationship between customer ratings and revenue generated, sized by quantity
Category Ranking Across Time – Rank movement of categories over successive time periods
Revenue Generating by Customers – Individual customer contribution to total revenue


Dashboard Features


Interactive filters for Category, Quantity, and Sub-Category to control the entire report
Packed bubble chart for category-wise revenue contribution
Treemap visualization for revenue distribution across menu item types
Scatter plot correlating customer rating against revenue, with bubble size representing quantity
Rank trend line chart tracking category position changes over time
Bubble timeline chart showing revenue generated by individual customers

Tools Used


Tableau Desktop – Dashboard design and visualization
Tableau Calculated Fields – Custom metrics for averages, sums, and rank calculations
Filters and Parameters – Interactive controls for category, quantity, and sub-category-based analysis


How to Use


Clone or download this repository.
Open the .twbx workbook file in Tableau Desktop or Tableau Reader.
Use the Category, Quantity, and Sub-Category filters at the top right of the dashboard to refine the view.
Hover over chart elements to view detailed tooltips with underlying values.
Scroll down to access additional visuals, including Category Ranking Across Time and Revenue Generating by Customers.
